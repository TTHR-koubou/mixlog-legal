Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Overview

Mixlog ("the App") is a recipe logging tool developed by Mixlog Team ("we", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

Mixlog stores all recipe data, photos, and preferences locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your recipe data on external servers.

Purchase Information

If you purchase Mixlog Pro, the transaction is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We use RevenueCat, a third-party service, to manage purchase verification. RevenueCat may process an anonymous user identifier and purchase receipt to validate your subscription. No personal information such as your name, email, or payment details is shared with us. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Photos

Photos you take or select within the App are stored only on your device. They are not uploaded to any server.

Analytics

The App does not currently use any analytics or tracking services. If we add analytics in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — Purchase management. Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, deleting the App will remove all associated data. We do not retain any data on our servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
